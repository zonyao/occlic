1. Fees Information Section

In this section you will be required to pay the necessary fees to submit the application. Please read the following instructions carefully.

  1. The following information has been prepopulated for you.
    1. License type (as selected in the type of license section)
    2. Fee for the license
  2. Steps to pay the Fees (If you have already made a payment on ePay and have a valid voucher number, you may enter the voucher number in without going back to ePay.)
    1. Click on the “Go to ePay” button
    2. The ePay website will open in a separate window
    3. On the ePay home page, click the "Other" link at the bottom of the page under "Option 3. View List of Fees By Media Type" to find Occupational Licensing options. Once on the "Other" page, under Occupational Licensing, select the type of license you are applying for. Follow the ePay instructions to make the payment
    4. Make a note of the voucher number provided by ePay
      5. Note: It is very important that you make a note of the voucher number correctly as this voucher number will be needed for submission within the online application
  3. Type the voucher number correctly in the voucher number box and click the ‘Next’ button
    1. If the voucher number is correct and has not been used previously, you will be navigated to the affidavit section where you can submit the online application
    2. If the voucher number is not correct or it has already been used, the system will show an error and will not allow you to proceed further until you submit a valid voucher number.
Note: For US Military Veterans: If you are a United States military veteran and you are applying for an initial license with the TCEQ, you are exempt from paying the initial application processing fee. Please check the box United States military veteran, upload the TCEQ Military Affidavit, and form DD-214 Certificate of Release or Discharge from Active Duty or DD-220 Active Duty Report paperwork in the Supporting documents section. To be eligible to receive this exemption you must have documented proof of United States Military Veterans, you are not eligible for a fee exception if you served in the military of any other country.
